Tip From the Kitchen!

When making your cupcake batter, don't mix the batter for too long!

Mixing batter for too long will result in a denser cupcake, and you will lose a lot of the fluffy-ness that everyone loves! For best results, do a bit of research on the ingredients you're using to find out the perfect amount of time for mixing.
